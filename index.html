2. Problem Description

In the ever-growing digital landscape, vast amounts of data are generated daily across various sectors. Our mini project focuses on solving the real-world challenge of predicting customer churn in subscription-based services. Churn prediction helps companies proactively retain customers by identifying behaviors and patterns that indicate cancellation intent.

Our solution leverages predictive analytics techniques such as logistic regression, decision trees, and ensemble models to classify and predict customer churn. Using real-world datasets, we preprocessed data, engineered meaningful features, and evaluated models using key performance metrics.

This project not only demonstrates technical implementation but also showcases the power of data in driving strategic business decisions.
